Privacy Policy for Autosift

Last Updated: December 25, 2025

Summary: Autosift allows you to filter and automate lead purchases on IndiaMart. We collect your email for account management and store usage counters (e.g., "5 leads used"). We DO NOT store the actual content of the leads (names, phone numbers) you purchase. That data stays on your computer.

1. Data We Collect

When you use the Autosift Chrome Extension, we process the following data:

  • Account Information: Your email address and encrypted password (managed securely by Supabase Authentication).
  • Usage Metrics: The number of leads you purchase daily to enforce plan limits.
  • Referral Data: Referral codes and links between user accounts to award bonus credits.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not store the Buyer's Name, Phone Number, or Requirement Text from the leads you view.
  • We do not track your browsing history outside of the IndiaMart Seller Panel.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

  • Authenticate your login.
  • Enforce your plan limits (e.g., 2 leads/day vs 100 leads/day).
  • Calculate referral bonuses.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party infrastructure:

  • Supabase: For secure database and authentication services.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via our support channels.
