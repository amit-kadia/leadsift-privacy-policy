Privacy Policy for Autosift

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

Autosift ("we", "our", or "us") provides a Chrome Extension designed to help B2B sellers filter leads efficiently. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information.

1. Data We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address and basic profile information (name, profile picture) to create your account and manage your usage limits.

Usage Data: We track the count of actions performed (e.g., number of leads purchased, hidden, or shortlisted) to enforce daily limits and prevent abuse.

Lead Data: The actual content of the leads you process (buyer names, phone numbers, requirements) is stored locally on your device using Chrome's Local Storage. We do not transmit this data to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and secure your account via Supabase (our backend provider).
  • To enforce daily usage limits associated with your plan.
  • To improve the extension's performance and fix bugs.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Google OAuth: For secure authentication.
  • Supabase: For database management and user authentication.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including Row Level Security (RLS) on our databases, to ensure your account data is protected. Lead data remains on your local machine.

5. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of your stored information
  • Delete Your Account: Permanently remove all your data from our servers
  • Withdraw Consent: Stop using the extension anytime
